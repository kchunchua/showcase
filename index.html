I have a background in design and digital marketing for 4 year-ish since I earned my master degree in Multimedia Technology and I know in my heart that I want to pursuit my career in this field.

I currently play a role of UX/UI Web Designer at ABROADERS Co.,Ltd, a Japanese company based in Bangkok, which provides a website solutions and maintenance service for its mother company in Japan. I have an opportunity to work on various projects; designing and front-end coding for Reeracoen Group branches websites (10 branches), landing pages, ad banners, of which I can complete within estimated timelines, milestones and deadlines.

I feel that UX/UI is not everything about art and graphics, it’s more of the way to solve people’s problem and help them to accomplish goals when using web application, products or even life! That’s what makes it so exciting to be in this field because there will always be something new to learn.
I received my Bachelor of Arts (English Major) from the faculty of Arts, Chulalongkorn University, Thailand and earned my master’s degree in Multimedia Technology from the Swinburne University of Technology, Australia.

Several of my courses and knowledge background directly prepared me for being a digital designer. Particularly my master’s degree in multimedia required extensive web/digital product execution and research including project management, user experience design, digital media production and communication practice. These courses gave me the real-world experience that I needed to know when I enter the digital technology industry.
My career roadmap is to focus on my long-term goal of becoming a design all-rounder and to lead projects at all stages in the design process. My interim goal for the next coming year is to gain practical experience in the broader UX field and find opportunities to help develop the strategy for UX projects within my current company.
