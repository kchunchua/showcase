Brief & Requirement

The main goal from the mother company, Reeracoen Group, was to redesign the existing website using for 10 branches of Reeracoen companies across Asia. The key feature for this renewal was to provide users the ability to register their career profile online and conveniently apply for jobs offered from the Reeracoen job pool which are related to their work experiences and skills. That will solve user’s problems and ultimately obtain more online application numbers.

Design Thinking Process

Empathize

The renew of Reeracoen website is arising from the pain point that users could not register their profile and skills onto the old version of the Reeracoen website. That made users waste time to apply for their interested jobs over and over again, and they need to fill in the same information for each job application and ended with the user left the registration page.

Mockup & Prototyping

Working closely with the project manager and developers to initiate wireframes and flows. We researched on recruitment competitors’ website and focused mostly on user-centric problems on profile registration, job applying process - on how to reduce process time. Because the core features were an online registration and user’s profile page, I needed to focus on a high quality and feasible UIs of signup, login, form filling and user’s profile page using Mobile-First method. Color and typography were used according to corporate’s existing color theme (blue color).

Delivery

Deliverables were all screens design across mobile/desktop devices and responsive front-end coding using HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript.
