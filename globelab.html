Brief & Requirement

Design and conceptualize the web-based landing page for GlobeLab, a project management tool application. The goal is to collect app download numbers on GlobeLab main website. Additionally, the prototype of ‘application onboarding' carousel screens was created by Lottie/BodyMovin plugin, developed by AirBnB, which will display an animation when the carousel dots are triggered.
